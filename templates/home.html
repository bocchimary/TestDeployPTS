Guidelines

Creating Account

For Current Students and Alumni with a Google account:
  • Current students and alumni are required to sign in with Google using their institutional email account.
  • Step 1: Go to Login.
  • Step 2: Click Sign in with Google.
  • Step 3: Sign in using your institutional email (@pts.edu.ph).
  • Step 4: Complete your profile information to finish the registration process.
For New Students and Alumni from 2020 and earlier:
  • Step 1: Go to Login.
  • Step 2: Select Sign Up as Student or Sign Up as Alumni, depending on your status.
  • Step 3: Fill out the signup form using a valid and active email address.
  • Step 4: After signing up, a 4-digit OTP code will be sent to your email.
  • Step 5: Enter the OTP code to verify your account.
  • Step 6: ✅ Wait for your account to be verified. An email notification will be sent once your account is approved.

Guidelines

This section provides step-by-step guidelines for common student processes such as requests, clearance, enrollment, and graduation. Each category is designed to help you understand the requirements, prepare the necessary documents, and follow the correct procedures with ease.

Creating Account

Student Sign Up
  • Step 1: Go to Login.
  • Step 2: Click Sign Up and choose Student.
  • Step 3: Fill out the form using your official school email (@pts.edu.ph).
  • Step 4: After signing up, an OTP will be sent to your email. Enter the 4-digit code provided by the system to verify your account.
Sign Up with Google
  • Step 1: Go to Login.
  • Step 2: Select Sign Up as Student.
  • Step 3: Use your official school Google account to sign up, then click Allow to continue.
  • Step 4: After signing in, complete your profile to finish the registration process.

Request Guidelines

  • Step 1: Fill out the request form.
  • Step 2: Submit to the Registrar’s Office.
  • Step 3: Wait for email confirmation.

Clearance Guidelines

  • Secure clearance form.
  • Have it signed by all departments.
  • Return completed clearance for verification.

Enrollment Guidelines

  1. Check the schedule of enrollment.
  2. Prepare required documents.
  3. Proceed to cashier for payment.
  4. Submit proof of payment and finalize enrollment.

Graduation Guidelines

  • Apply for graduation at the Registrar’s Office.
  • Complete clearance requirements.
  • Pay graduation fees.
  • Attend rehearsal and ceremony.

PTS College & Advanced Studies - Official

At PTS College & Advanced Studies (PTSCAS), we aim to streamline and simplify the credential request process through a reliable and user-friendly system. This platform is primarily designed for the Office of the Registrar to efficiently handle credential requests from both students and alumni.

Our Team

The PTSCAS Request Credentials Form System was developed by a group of researchers from the Bachelor of Engineering Technology major in Computer Engineering Technology program at TUP Cavite. The system further aims to streamline and simplify the process of requesting essential documents such as transcripts of records, diplomas, certifications, and more. In line with the credential requests, the system is also capable of handling the clearance, graduation, and enrollment process. By integrating these services into one platform, it helps students save time and reduces the hassle of manual transactions.
